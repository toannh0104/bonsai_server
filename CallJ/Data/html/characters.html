Characters
The following table lists the various characters that may appear in this application. It is important to know
both how refer to each character. Also, it may be vital to adjust your level of politeness depending
on who you are talking too.. For example, you would use polite Japanese when conversing with your
teacher, but perhaps plain Japanese when talking to a friend. The Relative Level field demonstrates
this. For example, a teacher has a relative level of "Higher", whereas a child has a level "lower".

Image Relationship Relative Level
Yourself -
Brother (older) Equal
Brother (younger) Equal
Sister (older) Equal
Sister (younger) Equal
Friend Equal
Teacher Higher
Child Lower
