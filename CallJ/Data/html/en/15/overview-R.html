"This, that, and the other"
Listing words and actions

In this lesson we will practise sentences which involve incomplete lists of actions or objects. We will first consider incomplete lists of nouns, and then move on to sentences which describe lists of actions. The main forms to be practised in this lesson are summarised by the following examples.

1Listing actions
ringo wo tabetari banana wo tabetari simasuI ate apples and ate bananas.
2Listing subjects and objects
basu ya takusi wa benri daBuses and taxis are convenient.
ko-ra ya orenjiju-su wo nondaI drank cola and orange juice.
3Listing phrases
terebi wo mitari sensei ni kiitari simasitaI watched TV, asked the teacher a question, and did other things.
okasi wo tabetari shukudai wo sitari suruI eat sweets, do my homework, and do other things.
4Listing predicates
shoubu de kattari maketari suruSometimes I win games and sometimes I lose.
