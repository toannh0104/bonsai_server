Expressing the relative order of actions


This lesson covers how to express the relative order of actions. First we will consider the case where an event occurs before or after another. Next, we will practise sentences where two events occur simultaneously. Finally, we will cover more advanced expressions for describing how often an event occurs or when it is expected to be completed.
1'before',
学校に行く前に水を飲んだBefore I go to the school, I drink water
2'after'
朝食を食べた後でテニスをしたAfter I had breakfast, I played tennis
3'then'
母が来てから朝食を食べるMy mother comes, and then we have breakfast
4'while'
テレビを見ながら新聞を読むI read a newspaper while I am watching the TV
5'during'
休みの間に映画館へ行くDuring holidays, I go to the theater
6'by'
明日までに仕事をするBy tomorrow, I finish the work
7frequency
一日に二回兄はミチコに電話しますTwo times a day, Michiko's brother call to her
