"It is easy to do'"
Expressing the ease or difficulty of an action


In this lesson we will practise making statements about the ease or difficulty of an action. This is done by adding a suffix to the stem form of a verb to change it into an adjective which expresses the ease or difficulty of the action described by the verb. The two main forms to be practised in this lesson are be summarised by the following examples.

1Easy vs Difficult
このペンは書きやすいですThis pen is easy to write
このペンは書きにすいですThis pen is difficult to write
2Polite vs Plain
この本は読みやすいですThis book is easy to read (Polite)
この本は読みやすいThis book is easy to read (Plain)
