Describing where an object or person is..


In this lesson we will look at how to describe the location of an (inanimate) object relative to another object. We will also look at how to say were a person (or other animate creature) is. Finally, there will also be questions regarding ownership, such as saying person X has object Y.

1Animate vs Inanimate
ジョンさんはぎんこうにいます"John is at the bank"
かぎはテーブルの上にあります"The key is on the table"
2Location vs Ownsership
ジョンさんはぎんこうにいます"John is at the bank"
ジョンさんはくるまがあります"John owns a car"
3Polite vs Plain
ねこはにわにいます"The cat is in the garden"
ねこはにわにいません"The cat is not in the garden"
4Polite vs Plain
サリーさんはこうえんにいます"Sally is at the park" (polite)
サリーさんはこうえんにいる"Sally is at the park" (plain)
5Non-past vs Past
サムさんはきっぷがあります"Sam has a ticket"
サムさんはきっぷがありました"Sam had a ticket"
6Statement vs Question
シャツはベッドのしたにあります"The shirt is under the bed"
シャツはベッドのしたにありますか"Is the shirt under the bed?"
