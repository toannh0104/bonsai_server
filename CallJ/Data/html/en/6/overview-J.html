"Shall we drink some tea?"
Offering invitations and suggestions

In this lesson we will look at how to invite someone to do something. We will also look at how to give suggestions.

1Statement vs Question
ビールを飲みましょう"Let's drink some beer."
ビールを飲みましょうか"Shall we drink some beer?"
2Polite vs Plain
今から行きましょう"Let's go now." (polite)
今から行こう"Let's go now." (plain)
今から行きましょうか"Shall we go now?" (polite)
今から行こうか"Shall we go now?" (plain)
* Note: All answers given in this lesson should be of the form(s) shown above.
