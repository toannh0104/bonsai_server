"Đây là một quyển sách'"
Usage of は, です,も,これ,それ (etc) to form basic statements about an object..


In this lesson we will practise making simple statements about objects, regarding what they basically are. We will also consider using pronouns to refer to objects closer to us than the listener (ie. "This book" etc), and those closer to the listener ("That book" etc). Finally, we will also consider the use of "も" as in "also". The main forms to be practised in this lesson can be summarized by the following examples.

1Objects vs People:
これはリンゴです"This is an apple" (object)
ジョンさんはいしゃです"John is a doctor" (person)
2Question vs Statement:
これはオレンジです"This is an orange?"
それはオレンジですか"Is that an orange?"
3Positive vs Negative:
サリーサンはイギリスじんです"Sally is English"
いまにしさんはイギリスじんではありません"Imanishi is not English"
