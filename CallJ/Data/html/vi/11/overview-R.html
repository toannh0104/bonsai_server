Going somewhere to do an action

In this lesson we will practise a grammar pattern used to express the purpose of going somewhere. In this type of sentence, the subject goes somewhere to perform an action like eating or shopping. The main forms to be practised in this lesson are summarised by the following examples.

1GoToDo
watashi ha ko-ra wo kainiikimasuI will go out to buy cola.
watashi ha osake wo nominiikimasuI will go out to drink alcohol.
