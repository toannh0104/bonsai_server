Comparisons

In this lesson we will study expressions used when making comparisons. First, simple expressions comparing for two things and expressing a preference between them will be introduced. Then, we will practise more complicated expressions for expressing a preference for one thing out of a group of things. You must pay attention to the size of the group when asking questions about preferences because different question words are used for different kinds of groups.
1Than
札幌は京都より寒い。Sapporo is colder than Kyoto.
2Too
今日は寒すぎる。Today, it is too cold.
3Which
これらのお酒のなかでどれが最も好きですか。Which do you like best in these alcohol drinks?
4What
お酒のなかで何が最も好きですか。What do you like best in these alcohol drinks?
5Which..., A or B
猫と犬とどちらが好きですか。Which do you prefer, cats or dogs?

* Note: All answers given in this lesson should be of the form(s) shown above.
