"This, that, and the other"
Listing words and actions

In this lesson we will practise sentences which involve incomplete lists of actions or objects. We will first consider incomplete lists of nouns, and then move on to sentences which describe lists of actions. The main forms to be practised in this lesson are summarised by the following examples.

1Listing actions
リンゴを食べたりバナナを食べたりしましたI ate apples and ate bananas.
2Listing subjects and objects
バスやタクシーは便利だBuses and taxis are convenient.
コーラやオレンジジュースを飲んだI drank cola and orange juice.
八百屋や喫茶店へ行くI will go to the vegetable shop and the coffee shop.
3Listing phrases
テレビを見たり先生に聞いたりしましたI watched TV, asked the teacher a question, and did other things.
お菓子を食べたり宿題をしたりするI eat sweets, do my homework, and do other things.
4Listing predicates
勝負で勝ったり負けたりするSometimes I win games and sometimes I lose.
