"You should rest"
Recommendations, rules, and suggestions

In this lesson we will cover expressions used for giving recommendations and expressing preference for one thing over another. We will also see how to express situations where the speaker or listener must do something. Finally, we will learn an expression for giving the listener permission to do something without specifying the details. The main forms to be practised in this lesson can be summarised by the following examples.

1Recommendations
hayaku kaetta hou ga iiYou should go home early.
watashi o amari okorasenai hou ga iiYou shouldn't make me so angry.
sochira no keikaku no hou ga iidesuThat plan is better.
2Obligations
gakkou ni ikanakereba naranaiI have to go to school.
benkyou o shinakereba naranaiI have to study.
otoko niwa yaranakereba naranai koto ga aruA man has to do what a man has to do.
3Permission
asu ittara iidesuYou can just go tomorrow.
sou shitara iidesuYou can do that.
zenbu anata no omou you ni sitara iidesuYou can do it all however you please.
