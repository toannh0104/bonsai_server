 Expressing the relative order of actions


This lesson covers how to express the relative order of actions. First we will consider the case where an event occurs before or after another. Next, we will practise sentences where two events occur simultaneously. Finally, we will cover more advanced expressions for describing how often an event occurs or when it is expected to be completed.
1'before',
gakkou ni iku maeni mizu wo nondaBefore I go to the school, I drink water
2'after'
tyoushoku wo tabeta atode tenisu wo shitaAfter I had breakfast, I played tennis
3'then'
haha ga kite kara tyoushoku wo taberuMy mother comes, and then we have breakfast
4'while'
terebi wo mi nagara shinbun wo yomuI read a newspaper while I am watching the TV
5'during'
yasumi no aida ni eigakan he ikuDuring holidays, I go to the theater
6'by'
asu madeni shigoto wo suruBy tomorrow, I finish the work
7frequency
itiniti ni ni kai ani ha mitiko nidenwa shimasuTwo times a day, Michiko's brother call to her
