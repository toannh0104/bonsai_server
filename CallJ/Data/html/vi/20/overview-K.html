Probability and uncertainty

In this lesson we will practise how to express varying degrees of uncertainty in sentences. When describing an uncertain event in Japanese, the expression to be used is decided by the information available, the source of that information, and how the speaker feels about the validity of that information. Many of the expressions can have similar English translations, so it is important to understand the nuances of each one.
