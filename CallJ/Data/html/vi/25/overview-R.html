"I think I can go tomorrow."
Expressions with subordinate clauses


This lesson focuses on expressions that use subordinate clauses marked by the particle "と". You can think of subordinate clauses as small sentences that are embedded in a larger sentence. We will practice expressions that use a subordinate clause to express what someone says or thinks. We will also learn an expression that describes or explains a noun without using a subordinate clause.
1Quotation, Thought(引用)
ikanai to ittaI said not to go
yakyu wo siyou to omouI think to play baseball　
asu ikeru to omoimasuI think I can go tomorrow.
imouto ga sukida to ittaMy younger sister said she likes it.　
ani to iku to ittaI said I'll go with my older brother.
otouto ga hosii to omoimasuI think my younger brother will want it
yakyuu wo suru to iuI'll say we will play baseball.
asoko he ikou to omoimasuI'm thinking about going over there.
yakyuu wo sitemitai to omouI want to try playing baseball.
ane ha benkyou wo suru to omouI think my older sister will study.
2Description(標識化)
