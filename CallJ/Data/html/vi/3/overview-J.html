Describing the location of a person or object

In this lesson we will look at how to describe the location of an (inanimate) object relative to another object. We will also look at how to say where a person (or other animate creature) is.

1Animate
ジョンさんは銀行にいます"John is at the bank."(Statement)
ジョンさんは銀行にいますか"Is John at the bank?"(Question)
ジョンさんは銀行にいません"John isn't at the bank."(Negative)
ジョンさんは銀行にいる"John is at the bank."(Plain)
ジョンさんは銀行にいました"John was at the bank."(Past)
2Inanimate
鍵はテーブルの上にあります"The key is on the table."(Statement)
鍵はテーブルの上にありますか"Is the key on the table?"(Question)
鍵はテーブルの上にありません"isn't the key on the table?"(Negative)
鍵はテーブルの上にある"The key is on the table."(Plain)
鍵はテーブルの上にありました"The key was on the table."(Past)
