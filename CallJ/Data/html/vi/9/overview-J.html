Expressing reasons and giving explanations


In this lesson we will practice how to express reasons ans explanations. To express reasons, you can uses から(kara) and ので(node). These words were introduced by a conditional clause. To express explanations, you can use だ(da) or です(desu)。In this case, these words were introduced by euphony ん(n). e.g.なんだ。(na n da.)
1Reasons with Kara
暑いからコーラを飲む。Since it's hot, I drink cola.
2Reasons with Node
ジョンは忙しいのでサッカーをしない。John won't play soccer because he's busy.
日曜日なので教会へ行く。Because it's Sunday, I'll go to church.
3Explanation with N-da/desu
ジョンの鞄は大きいんです。John's bag is big.
明日ジョンに会うんです。I'll meet John tomorrow.
夕飯はピザなんです。We're having pizza for dinner.
